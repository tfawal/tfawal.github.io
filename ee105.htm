  • EE105 Reader

In the summer of 2022 I helped to edit and revise Professor Ali Niknejad's EE105 Reader for the introductory microelectronics leader course at Cal. I took the course with Professor Niknejad in the Fall 2021 semester. This project is being taken on for two primary reasons. The first is to help reinforce my understanding of the material in preparation for EE140 (Analog Integrated Circuits), which I will be taking in the Fall of 2022 semester. The second reason is to help any future students that will be taking EE105 with Ali.

This will be an ongoing project, and I will be attending the EE105 lectures this semester in order to further understand the material, and make revisions to each chapter as the class goes through them. I believe this will also get my finger on the pulse of how students are understanding the material, and what areas might need improving.
